What is the transfusion blood volume calculator?
transfusion blood volume calculator is a tool that determines the blood transfusion volume for children required to achieve the aimed hemoglobin increment.
How to calculate transfusion blood volume?
To calculate transfusion blood volume you should use the following formula:
transfusion blood volume = weight * desired hemoglobin increment * 3 / hematocrit
